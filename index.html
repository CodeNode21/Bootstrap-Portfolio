About me
Hi! I'm Mat. I'm all about personal growth and helping people out any way I can. Whether it'd be personal fitness, finances, nutrition or if you're having a bad day, I'm always up for a chat. Currently I'm full time accountant while I also teach swimming in the evenings. You might wonder what got me into web development- well, I'll tell you. Curiosity has pushed me down a rabbit hole. While working in accounting I realized that there were many repetitive excel tasks that could be done by creating macros. Watching excel record all my moves on it's sheet I figured I could potentially learn how to do that outside excel. So I picked up a book and stareted learning Python. Realizing all the possibilities my mind was blown and I was hooked. Currently I'm learning HTML, CSS and soon JS. I just can't wait to have a full grip on these languages. Cool things coming soon...
